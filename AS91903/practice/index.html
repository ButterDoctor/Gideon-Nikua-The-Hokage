GIDEON THE GANGSTA HOKAGE PROFILE

My name is Gideon Nikua, but my friends refer to me as Gideon Uzumaki the Hokage. I believe that in every person there is good that can be salvaged, no matter how great the pain is. Seeing my companions getting hurt unleashes the fox like beast that resides in me. But I try my best to keep the beast at bay, but once it is unleashed, everyone will know how dangerous I really am.
